We collect personal identification information (name, email, phone, DOB), academic and counselling information (JEE Advanced AIR, category, preferences), payment information (processed securely via Razorpay), and communication data (chat logs with Saathi, mentor session notes).

2. How We Use Your Information

To provide personalised counselling, generate preference lists, process payments, send JoSAA deadline alerts, improve our AI's accuracy, and respond to support queries.

3. Data Sharing & Protection

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal data. We share data only with necessary service providers (Razorpay, Google, WhatsApp API) and assigned human mentors bound by NDAs. Your data is encrypted and stored securely.

Saathi is an automated AI tool. It is not a licensed counsellor. Responses may contain errors. We accept no liability for decisions made based on Saathi's responses. All information must be independently verified from official JoSAA sources.
